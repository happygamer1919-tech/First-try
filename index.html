About the Shelter

Volunteer-led rescue, rehabilitation, and lifelong care. Transparent and community-backed. We focus on sustainable, ethical care and turn every donation into warmth, food, safety, and medical care.

How you can help

Volunteer at the shelter

Hands-on help with feeding, cleaning, cuddles, and walks. Time is love.

Foster a dog temporarily

Give a short-term home to dogs healing from surgery, stress, or cold.

Donate supplies

Food, toys, blankets, meds—everything helps. We use every item.

Share our stories

Spread the word on social—visibility brings homes and help.

Sponsor monthly

Keep the basics covered with small recurring support. Predictable help saves lives.

Want to help as a business sponsor or organize a fundraiser? We’d love to talk.

FAQ

Where do my donations go?

Food, vet bills, shelter maintenance, and emergency rescues.

Can I visit the shelter?

Yes—please contact us first so we can arrange a safe time for the dogs.

Do you accept item donations?

Absolutely. Blankets, food, toys, medicine—everything is appreciated.
